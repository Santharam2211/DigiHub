# Getting Started Guide - Event Management System

Welcome to the Event Management System! This guide will help new users understand how to use the application step by step.

## 🎯 What is this System?

This is a web platform where you can:
- **Register for events** - Sign up for workshops, seminars, competitions, and more
- **Download event passes** - Get QR code passes for event entry
- **Track your registrations** - See all events you've signed up for
- **Apply for leadership positions** - Submit nominations for association roles
- **Download certificates** - Receive certificates after attending events

## 📱 First Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Open the Application**
   - Go to the application URL (e.g., `http://localhost:5173`)
   - You'll see the login page

2. **Click "Create Free Account"**
   - This takes you to the registration form

3. **Fill in Your Information**

   **Personal Details:**
   - Full Name: Enter your real name
   - Student/Reg ID: Your college registration number (optional but recommended)
   - Date of Birth: Select your birth date
   - Gender: Select your gender

   **Academic Information:**
   - Year & Department: Choose your current year and department
     - Years: I, II, III, IV (B.E.)
     - Departments: CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, IT, AI&DS, etc.
   - Class Section: Select your section (A, B, C, or Nil)

   **Contact Information:**
   - Email: Use a valid email you check regularly
   - Phone: Your contact number (optional)
   - Password: Create a password (minimum 6 characters)

   **About You:**
   - Bio: Write a short description about yourself
   - Skills: List your skills (e.g., "JavaScript, Python, Design")

4. **Set Security Questions (IMPORTANT!)**
   
   You must answer these three questions for password recovery:
   - Best Friend's Name (one word)
   - Favorite Color (one word)
   - Favorite Hero Name (one word)
   
   ⚠️ **Remember these answers!** You'll need them if you forget your password.

5. **Complete Registration**
   - Click "Create Participant Account"
   - You'll be automatically logged in

### Step 2: Explore Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard with:

**Welcome Banner**
- Shows your name with a greeting
- Special birthday banner on your birthday

**My Nominations**
- Shows any leadership positions you've applied for
- Status indicators: Green (Approved), Red (Rejected), Amber (Pending)

**My Registrations**
- Lists all events you've registered for
- Shows event details, dates, and venues
- Download buttons for event passes

## 🎪 How to Register for Events

### 1. Browse Available Events

- Click on "Events" in the navigation menu
- Browse through the event cards
- Use filters to find events by category or date

### 2. View Event Details

- Click on any event card to see details:
  - Event description
  - Date and time
  - Venue/location
  - Registration deadline
  - Fee (if applicable)
  - Maximum capacity

### 3. Register for an Event

- Click "Register Now" on the event page
- Fill out the registration form:
  - Your basic info is pre-filled
  - Complete any custom fields required by the event
  - Upload any required files
- Click "Submit Registration"
- You'll receive a confirmation message

### 4. Download Your Event Pass

- Go to your Dashboard
- Find the event in "My Registrations"
- Click "Download Pass"
- A QR code pass will be generated as an image
- Save this to your phone or print it

## 🎫 Using Your Event Pass

### At the Event Entrance

1. **Show Your QR Code**
   - Open the downloaded pass image on your phone
   - Or show a printed copy

2. **Get Scanned**
   - Event staff will scan your QR code
   - This marks your attendance automatically

3. **What's on Your Pass:**
   - Your name and photo
   - Event details (name, date, venue)
   - Your registration ID
   - QR code for verification

## 📋 Applying for Leadership Positions (Nominations)

### Check if Nominations are Open

- Go to the "Nominate" page
- If nominations are disabled, you'll see a message
- Only available when enabled by administrators

### Submit a Nomination

1. **Choose a Nomination Form**
   - Select the position you want to apply for
   - Read the description and requirements

2. **Fill the Nomination Form**

   **Personal Information:**
   - Name and email (pre-filled)
   - Registration number
   - Phone number

   **Academic Details:**
   - Current CGPA
   - Year and department
   - Section

   **Innovation Proposal:**
   - Title of your proposal
   - Detailed description
   - Expected outcomes

3. **Submit the Form**
   - Review all information
   - Click "Submit Nomination"
   - You'll receive a confirmation

### Track Your Nomination Status

- Go to your Dashboard
- Check "My Nominations" section
- Status updates through these stages:
  - **Submitted** → Waiting for Class Coordinator review
  - **Pending Class Coordinator** → Under review by your CC
  - **Pending Program Coordinator** → Under review by PC
  - **Pending Admin** → Final review by Administrator
  - **Approved** → Congratulations! Your nomination is successful
  - **Rejected** → Unfortunately, your nomination was declined

## 🏆 Receiving Certificates

### How Certificates Work

- Certificates are generated after events are completed
- Only participants who attended the event receive certificates
- Certificates are sent to your registered email
- Format: PDF file

### Download Certificates

- Go to your Dashboard
- Check "My Registrations"
- If a certificate is available, you'll see a download button
- Click to download the PDF

## 👤 Managing Your Profile

### Access Your Profile

- Click on your profile picture or name
- Select "Profile" from the menu

### Update Your Information

**Personal Tab:**
- Update your name, phone, date of birth
- Change your bio and skills
- Upload a profile picture
- Upload your signature (for official documents)

**Academic/Work Tab:**
- Update your registration number
- Change your year and department (limited changes)

**Security Tab:**
- Change your password
- Keep your account secure

## 🔐 Forgot Your Password?

If you forget your password, you can recover it using your security questions:

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Click "Forgot Password?"

2. **Enter Your Email**
   - Type your registered email address
   - Click "Continue"

3. **Answer Security Questions**
   - Answer ALL THREE questions correctly
   - Answers must match exactly what you entered during registration
   - Click "Verify Answers"

4. **Set New Password**
   - Enter your new password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm the new password
   - Click "Reset Password"

5. **Login with New Password**
   - Go back to login page
   - Use your email and new password

⚠️ **Important**: If you can't remember your security answers, contact your Class Coordinator or Administrator.

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I register for multiple events?**
A: Yes! You can register for as many events as you want.

**Q: What if I can't attend after registering?**
A: Contact the event coordinator. You may need to cancel your registration.

**Q: How do I know if I got a certificate?**
A: Check your Dashboard under "My Registrations". If available, you'll see a download button.

**Q: Can I change my department after registration?**
A: Department changes are limited. Contact your administrator if you need to change.

**Q: What if my QR code doesn't scan?**
A: Ensure the image is clear and not blurry. Try zooming in or showing a printed copy.

**Q: How long are my registrations saved?**
A: Your registration history is saved permanently in the system.
